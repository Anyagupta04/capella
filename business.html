Roadmap Plan

Expansion from Metro to Tier-2 Cities

A phased launch strategy mitigating operational risk and scaling network growth systematically.

Phase 1: Metro Launch (2026)

Rollout operations across 3 major Tier-1 metro capitals. Recruit first wave of 1,000 safety-accredited driver partners. Establish brand dominance and core security compliance protocols.

Phase 2: Tier-2 Expansion (2027)

Expand to 15 tier-2 suburban cities. Partner with local women self-help collectives to source drivers. Rollout localized pricing, pooled auto-rickshaws, and training hubs.

Phase 3: Multi-Modal Integration (2028)

Integrate clean energy e-bikes, micro-mobility, and public transit matching feeds into Capella app. Initiate female-only logistics and emergency transport services.

Phase 4: Safety Licensing API (2029)

License Capella's safety algorithms (Route Guard, SOS feed compression, Smart Buddy chatbot AI engine) as SaaS APIs to global logistics, courier, and transit brands.
